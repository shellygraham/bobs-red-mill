Orange Goji Smoothie

Makes 1 2½-cup serving

Ingredients:

  • 2 scoops Bob’s Red Mill Vanilla Protein Powder Nutritional Booster
  • 1 small orange
  • 3 tablespoons raw cashews
  • 1 tablespoon goji berries
  • ½ teaspoon wheatgrass powder or greens powder
  • 3/4 cup coconut water
  • 1¼ cup ice

Directions:

Use a microplane or fine grater to create ½ teaspoon (packed) of fresh orange zest, and place into a blender. Remove and discard the remaining peel, and add the orange to the blender. Add all the remaining ingredients except the ice, and blend until smooth. Add the ice and blend once more until frosty.
